How Speaker Notes Work

Speaker notes are parsed from your markdown file using H1 headings (#) as slide separators.

# Title Slide
Welcome everyone to my presentation

# First Content Slide
Here's what we'll cover today...
- Point 1
- Point 2

# Second Content Slide  
More notes here...
  • Each # Heading starts a new slide's notes
  • Content under each H1 belongs to that slide
  • The first H1 and its content = Slide 1
  • Continue with more H1s for additional slides

Tip: Make sure the number of H1 headings matches your PDF slide count!
